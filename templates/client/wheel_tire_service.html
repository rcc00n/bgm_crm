Service overview

Complete tire and wheel services for every driver

Worn tires, poor alignment, and unbalanced wheels do more than make your ride rough. They cost you money in fuel, cause uneven wear, and can be unsafe on wet or icy roads.

Bad Guy Motors offers full tire and wheel service for drivers across Medicine Hat. We mount, balance, and align using proper procedures so your tires wear evenly and your vehicle feels solid on the road.

We also specialize in oversized, aftermarket, and custom wheel setups. If the fitment is a bit different from stock, we are used to dealing with it.

  • Tire mounting and balancing
  • Seasonal tire changes and rotations
  • Wheel alignment and steering calibration
  • Flat repair and tire inspections
  • Oversized and aftermarket wheel fitment
  • TPMS sensor replacement and reset
